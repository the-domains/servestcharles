Serve St. Charles Non-Profit

Serve St. Charles was born as the local mission arm of Refuge Church in St. Charles, Missouri. We are a non-profit that serves the people and community of St. Charles, Missouri. Supported by Refuge Church and Ridgecrest Baptist Church we are made possible by our wonderful volunteers.
